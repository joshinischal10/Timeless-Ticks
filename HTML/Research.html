The inspiration for the navigation bar was referenced from a youtube video by CodingNepal. The use of in-line blocks and un-ordered lists to form the navigation bar was inspired from the video whereas the responsive element of the reference code was discarded. Home

Reference page Our page

The inspiration for the home page was taken from daraz, the top e-commerce platform of Nepal. The banner element along with the product selection and image slider were taken as reference from daraz's homepage whereas the sub-banner was discarded. The home page of timeless ticks shows various sections for other pages in the website which isn't present in the referenced webpage.
