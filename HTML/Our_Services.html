Return & Exchange

To exchange or return an item:
  1. Go to your orders and select return or replace items beside the item(s) you want to exchange.
  2. Select a reason for return or exchange.
  3. Follow the instructions on screen.
Note:
  • Items purchased with a promotion like discounts do not quality for an exchange.
  • Exchanges are only eligible for orders paid from online payment methods.
